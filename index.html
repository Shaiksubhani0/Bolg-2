Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish,perfect for any meal.This classic omelette combines beaten eggs
cooked to perfection,optionally filled with your choice of cheese,vegetables,or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation:5 minutes
  • Cokking:5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt,to taste
  • Pepper,to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • optional fillings:cheese,diced vegetables,cooked meatls,herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs:
    2. In a bowl,beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and peper until they are
well mixed.You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffer texture.
  2. Heat the pan:
    3. Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or all.
  3. Cook the omelette:
    4. Once the butter is melted and bubbling,pour in the eggs.Tlit then
pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add fillings (optional):
    5. When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly
runny in the middle,sprinkle your chosen filings over one half of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve:
    6. As the omelette continues to cook,carefully lift one edge and fold it
over the fillings.Let it cook for another minutes,then slide it onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy:
    7. Serve hot,with additional salt and peper if needed.
